Duke OrgMatcher

The Duke OrgMatcher is a personal profiler that matches you with ideal organizations based on your personal interests and past experiences. After you have completed this quick questionnaire, our system will search the database of over 200 student organizations and find the ones that match your interests on the most levels. You can learn more about these matching groups by reading their profiles, visiting their web sites, and contacting their representatives.
